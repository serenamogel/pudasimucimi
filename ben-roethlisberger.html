Biography

American Ben Roethlisberger is also known as Big Ben who is a two-time Super Bowl winner. In addition, Benjamin is a quarterback for the NFL. Presently, he was ranked all-time 9th in the NFL.

Ben Roethlisberger’s Early Life, and Education

Ben was born on the 2nd of March 2 in the year 1982, in Lima, Ohio, United States. He was raised by his father Kenneth Todd Roethlisberger and his mother Ida Jane Foust. His family comes from Anglo-Swiss-German ancestry.

His birth name is Benjamin Todd Roethlisberger Sr. and he grew up together with a sister, Carlee Roethlisberger, and a brother, Bob Roethlisberger. He holds an American nationality and has a white ethnic background.

Talking about his education, Ben is a graduate of Findlay High School in Findlay, Ohio, where he got experience as a captain in football, basketball, and baseball teams. For his further studies, he joined Miami University continuing his passion for football. He played three seasons from 2001-2003 and made a career path from it.

However, in the first week of 2008, he broke his record and completed 13 of 14 passes for a career-high 92.9 completion percentage. Making his past record, he won against the Texans with 137 yards passing and two touchdown passes in the 38–17 win.

Also same season he won Super Bowl champion for the second time. In Super Bowl XLIII, a football veteran made 21 of 30 for 256 yards with one touchdown, one interception, and a passer rating of 93.2.
