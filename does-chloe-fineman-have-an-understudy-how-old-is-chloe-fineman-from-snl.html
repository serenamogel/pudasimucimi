Does Chloe Fineman Have An Understudy? How Old Is Chloe Fineman From SNL?

Publish date: 2024-05-07

American actress Chloe Fineman actually has her own understudy, even though she is a full time understudy for Saturday Night Live (SNL).

“What happens if i get sick? Well, I actually have my own understudy who does a perfect impression of me”, she once said during an interview.

How Old Is Chloe Fineman From SNL?

Chloe Fineman is 34 years old. She was born on July 20, 1988.

Who Is Chloe Fineman?

Chloe Fineman is an actress, writer, and comedian, born in Berkeley, California, USA.

Fineman attended Piedmont High School, where she began to exhibit her talent in comedy and drama.

She began directing plays while in high school. Some of them include Metamorphoses by Mary Zimmerman; and The Vagina Monologues by Eve Ensler.

Fineman proceeded to study at the New York University Tisch School of the Arts and  the Stella Adler Studio.

In 2019,  she appeared on the 45th season of the NBC sketch-comedy show Saturday Night Live, and won one of the season’s MVP by Entertainment Weekly.

Fineman has been able to garner a number of celebrity impressions on SNL from the likes of Drew Barrymore, Carole Baskin, Nicole Kidman, Timothée Chalamet, Tiffany Trump, Britney Spears, Miley Cyrus, Megan Fox, Marilyn Monroe, Nancy Pelosi, and JoJo Siwa.
