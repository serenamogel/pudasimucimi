Amita Suman Family Nationality and Ethnicity

Did you know Amita Suman was born in Nepal? She was born in the rural municipality of Kalikarnai, previously known as Bhedihari VDC of Parsa District.

Although she has decided to keep her parents out of the reach of paparazzi and social media, it is known that they are both of Nepali Origin. 

She was born to a Bhojpuri-Speaking Family and went to Saint Xavier School in Birjung until her family immigrated to Brighton, England, when she was seven, searching for a quality life.

According to her Wikipedia, Suman had no indoor plumbing, education, or electricity during her childhood in Nepal. Her family had to grow their food, and they used hay for insulation.

Upon moving to England, Amita struggled to make new friends during her initial years due to cultural differences and the language barrier. 

The 26 years old Actress attended Sussex Downs College and later went to the Academy of Live and Recorded Arts in 2018 to improve her acting skills.
