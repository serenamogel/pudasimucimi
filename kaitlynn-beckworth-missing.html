Kaitlynn Beckworth Missing: Has She Been Found?

Publish date: 2024-02-02

Kaitlyn Beckworth, a 15-year-old girl hailing from the United States of America has garnered attention due to her disappearance. Because she was known for having close ties with her family, her disappearance has caused her loved ones to be upset and worried. This post has everything you need to know about her disappearance.

When Did Kaitlynn Beckworth Went Missing?

In the early hours of Saturday, August 19, 2023, 15-year-old Kaitlynn Beckworth was reported missing. She was last seen in Rockwall, Texas, in the 200 Block of Freedom Court before 1 a.m. Her family reported her missing because they were worried about her safety.

So, the Rockwall Police Department chose to take the case to the next level and asked the Texas Department of Public Safety to send out a regional Amber Alert. After this request was accepted, an Amber Alert was sent out to let people know that Kaitlynn had gone missing and ask for help in finding her.

Has Kaitlynn Beckworth Been Found Now?

The Amber Alert that had been issued for 15-year-old Kaitlynn Beckworth had been canceled on August 20, 2023, at 10:00 AM because she had been safely located. After she was found a tweet was shared with a caption, “DISCONTINUED LOCAL AMBER ALERT for Kaitlynn Beckworth from Rockwall, TX, on 08/20/2023.”
