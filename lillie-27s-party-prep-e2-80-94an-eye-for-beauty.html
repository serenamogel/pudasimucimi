Lillie's Party PrepAn Eye for Beauty

Publish date: 2024-08-17

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Lillie's Party Prep—An Eye for Beauty (Japanese: リーリエ編美しき衣装 Lillie Edition: Beautiful Outfits) was a costume event in Pokémon Masters EX, and a sub-story of the costume event Curious Tea Party. It was focused on the sync pair Lillie (Special Costume) & Polteageist.

The "Lillie Costume Scout" gave players an increased chance of recruiting Lillie (Special Costume) & Polteageist for the duration of the event.

During this event, players had to use a Story Key, which could be obtained by completing a story area in Curious Tea Party, to unlock the event's area info. Once unlocked, players could earn Event Vouchers R from battles that could be redeemed in the shop. All battles could be challenged an unlimited number of times except Daily Extreme Battle 1, which could only be challenged once per day. Extra vouchers and Story Keys were automatically exchanged with coins once the event was finished.

Players could battle with Lillie (Special Costume) & Polteageist, Sonia (Special Costume) & Tsareena, Ingo (Special Costume) & Accelgor, or Emmet (Special Costume) & Escavalier to obtain +0.6× bonus vouchers; Wallace & Milotic to obtain +0.4× bonus vouchers; and any unlisted sync pairs with the Alola or Galar theme skills to obtain +0.2× bonus vouchers.

This event featured a special riddle mission. In Fluttery Fashion, Wallace presented Lillie with a riddle, which the player would have to answer. The player could give their answer by completing the battle "Solve Wallace's Riddle!" using sync pairs with specific theme skills. Answering the riddle correctly would unlock How's My Outfit?, in which Lillie gave the same answer.

During the second run of Curious Tea Party, this event's story and battles were combined with the other sub-stories into Solving Wonderful Mysteries.
