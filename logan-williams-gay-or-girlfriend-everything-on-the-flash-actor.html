Logan Williams Gay Or Girlfriend: Everything On The Flash Actor

Publish date: 2024-09-25

Was The Flash actor Logan Williams gay, or did he have a girlfriend? Here’s what we know about the deceased actor.

Logan Williams was a child actor who died on 2 April 2020.

He was best known for his work on the TV series The Flash as young Barry Allen in 2014. Having given the first audition in 2013, he had worked on five projects before his passing.

Aside from The Flash, his other works include When calls The Heart and Supernatural, among others. He was also the recipient of two Joey Awards, one in 2014 and another in 2016.

Logan Williams Gay Or Girlfriend

Logan Williams wasn’t gay.

However, The Flash actor Rick Cosnett did come out as gay the same year Logan died. He announced his sexuality through an Instagram post on 14 February 2020.

As for Logan’s girlfriend, nothing significant was revealed before his death. And even after his death, nothing has been disclosed on the matter.

Further, his mother, Marlyse Williams, has said that her son enjoyed acting. Probably, he was solely focused on his career and didn’t make a girlfriend, but who knows.

Logan Williams Age And Cause Of Death

Logan Williams died at the age of 16 on 2 April 2021.

He apparently died of a fentanyl overdose. A 2020 preliminary toxicology report had stated that Logan’s death was caused by accidental acute drug toxicity. The latest report from Canada’s BC Coroners Service has confirmed the cause to be unintentional illicit drug toxicity.

Before his death, Logan had been dealing with addictions. He also had suffered from mental health issues. Moreover, he was in the Ministry of Children and Family Development’s care in 2019.

He was once found unresponsive on 26 February 2020 due to substance abuse, leaving him with brain injury affecting his functioning. Discharged on 11 March, he left the care home and was missing on 1 April. He then returned to the care home under the influence of a substance and went to sleep.

But, he never woke up from the deep slumber.
