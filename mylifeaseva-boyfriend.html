MyLifeAsEva Boyfriend Olav Stubberud Is A Photographer

During his work period in The Reveal Group, he was also the personal photographer for G-Easy.

Olav has also worked as a Creative Director at the basic. Besides, he is currently working full-time as Content Creator & Creative Director and is a freelancer at various work.

He began to work as a Freelancer as a YSL Beauty ambassador at Saint Laurent in January 2021 till now. He is the only male YSL beauty ambassador for Scandinavia.

Olav has worked as a Freelance Personal Photographer and Videographer at One Race Films since January 2021. He has also worked as a freelance ambassador and brand consultant in Douchebags since April 2022.

His work is to design products and consultant brands. Similarly, he is also the co-designer of the Ramverk product line.

He has also dedicated an Instagram account, @basic.byolav. The account has 15.9 k followers, and his posts get good views and likes.
