About

Famous for her membership in the politically prominent Nehru-Gandhi family, Priyanka Gandhi is the daughter of Politician Sonia Gandhi and former Indian Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi. Her brother, Rahul Gandhi, took office in 2013 as Vice-President of the Indian National Congress party.

Before Fame

She attended Delhi University.

Trivia

She served as campaign manager for her mother during the 2004 Indian general election.

Family Life

Her marriage to businessman Robert Vadra resulted in children named Raihan and Miraya.

Associated With

Her grandmother Indira Gandhi was the first (and, as of 2015, only) female Prime Minister of India.
