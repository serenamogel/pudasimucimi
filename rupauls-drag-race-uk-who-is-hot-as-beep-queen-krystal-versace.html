RuPauls Drag Race UK: Who is hot as beep queen Krystal Versace? Tue 31 August 2021 16:

Publish date: 2024-08-21

RuPaul will be putting twelve drag queens to the ultimate test again this September 2021 for another season of Drag Race UK.

Krystal Versace will be one of the contestants battling it out to take the crown… but who is this “hot as beep” queen?

From their background in drag, to their age and inspiration for entering the third season, Reality Titbit has explored all viewers need to know.

Who is Krystal Versace?

Krystal Versace is a 19-year-old drag queen from Kent!

They began drag two years ago when she was only 17. However, has been wearing makeup since she was 12 – but her looks were “not it”, as they say they used grey for bronzer…

Luckily this “s*x goddess” Queen has improved their skills since then and is now ready to take on Drag Race UK season three

Knowing they would be doing drag for the rest of their life – since their first-ever drag show – and described it as falling into something “magical, it was definitely meant to be for Krystal Versace.

They are a quarter Cypriot, while their Greek Grandma has given them the inspiration to go into the competition with the “opa!” attitude!

Krystal Versace on Drag Race

Drag queen, Krystal Versace, believes she will win drag race because she is both “different” and “refreshing”

She describes herself as a “huge goofball”, “the full package” and has said she’s not leaving without a crown – so this should get interesting!

Krystal Versace not only wants to win, but she also wants to inspire the next generation of drag.

Although they are only 19, they do not believe their age will hold them back in the competition, as they are an “unexpected surprise”.

They have been inspired by RuPaul’s Drag Race since young, so we can only imagine how much of a dream come true this is for them to be competing!
